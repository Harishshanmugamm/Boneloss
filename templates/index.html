About Us

Welcome to our Tooth Bone Loss Calculator!

Our mission is to provide a comprehensive tool for estimating tooth bone loss, offering treatment plans, and visualizing dental conditions.

Features:

Our application offers the following key features:

  • Tooth Bone Loss Estimation: Accurately estimate and monitor tooth bone loss over time.
  • Treatment Plans: Receive personalized treatment recommendations based on bone loss assessment.
  • Teeth Condition Visualization: View tooth conditions after 1st, 2nd, and 3rd weeks, and a monthly diagnostic report for tracking changes.

Benefits:

Using our Tooth Bone Loss Calculator provides several benefits:

  • Early Detection: Detect potential tooth bone loss at an early stage for proactive treatment.
  • Customized Treatment: Receive tailored treatment plans based on accurate bone loss assessments.
  • Progress Tracking: Visualize and monitor changes in tooth conditions to assess treatment effectiveness.

How It Works:

To estimate tooth bone loss, enter specific tooth details and initial bone loss assessment. Our system will provide a customized treatment plan based on the assessment. The teeth condition visualization feature allows you to track changes over time, helping in better understanding and management of dental health.
